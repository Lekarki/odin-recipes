Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mix
  4. Layer the lasagna
    1. meat
    2. noodles
    3. ricotta
    4. mozz
    5. meat
    6. parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let rest before serving
